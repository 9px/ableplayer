Able Player (UMP) Test #10:
Video with no captions or description

Why would you use an accessible media player to deliver inaccessible media? Makes no sense. But if you have a compelling reason (e.g., you have to share the video with a small private audience while waiting for captions and descriptions to be finished) this test shows that UMP is capable of handling it.
